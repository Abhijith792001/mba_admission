Dual Degree

Programme with University at Buffalo Amrita School of Business, Bengaluru offers a unique MBA programme in collaboration with one of the top public universities in USA – University at Buffalo, State University of New York (UB).

The Amrita–UB MBA-MS collaborative dual-degree programme began in 2009 and has taken great strides over the last few years. On successful completion, the graduand is conferred an MBA degree by Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham and an MS in Business Analytics and Systems by University at Buffalo.
